About Me

Hello! I am Garam Lee from Korea.
I am studying Human-Computer Interaction(HCI) in SKKU.
I really interested in observing people's behavior and thinking,
and I always try to keep thinking how can improve the User Expriences.

Expecially, these days, I am doing a project to make more accessible interaction among blind people, computer, and media.
I think studying other people's experiences especially that I almost totally I haven't experienced before is really fascinating.
So I really love doing this work and for about 6 months, I met and interviewed blind people and related field's experts to know their real use of IT device.

Except just this project, I always try to keep open my eyes and ears to see and hear what happen and what people think.
